===Biography
---
Chien-Liang Chen received the Ph.D. degree in Electrical Engineering from National Taiwan University in 2009. He was an assistant professor of Tourism Information from February 2010 to July 2012 and an assistant professor of Computer Science and Information Engineering at Aletheia University from August 2012 to July 2014. From August 2014, he is an associate professor of Computer Science and Information Engineering at Aletheia University. His current research interests include Optimization Algorithms, Wireless Sensor Networks and Discrete Event Systems.
